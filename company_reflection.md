## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was created to help people with ADHD/ASD to stay focus and the team felt that the existing tools didn't work well for neurodivergent brains

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Focus Bear is solving the problem that people cant stay focus, getting distracted by other apps or ding unnecessary things they should be doing at the time. They are also improving people's daily habits such as morning workout
## Why do you think this mission is important?
Digital distractions are everywhere, and for many  people especially for people who have ADHD/ADS it can derail them from study/work and wellbeing. The apps biscally blocks distraction and helps people shape routines and recovery breaks can make productivity more sustainable instead of exhausting
